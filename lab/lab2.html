Temporary Financial Assistance Available

To be eligible for this program, you must meet the following criteria:
  1. Have a child with a physical or mental disability diagonised within the last 6 months (the diagonise can be prenatal or at any age)
  2. Be at below the state of New Mexico poverty line
To apply, please do the following:
  1. Pick up an application from ChildLink (refer to link above)
  2. Assemble the following paper:
    1. Your completed application
    2. Doctor's records oof your child diagosis
    3. Tax records or New Mexico Social Services certificate of your income level
    4. Your lease, mortage, or medical bills, depending on which you need help with
  3. Make an appointment with a ChildLink volunteer, available these times:
    • Ida: MW 10:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
    • TR 9:00a.m. to noon
    • Chris: F 10:30a.m. to 3:30p.m.
